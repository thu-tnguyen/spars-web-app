About us

We’re the Student Project and Research Symposium Club of Orange Coast College. As a community of aspired researchers and developers who are actively participating in OCC’s annual research symposium, we set out to promote and fortify the student research community of OCC with resources and workshops, namely peer review sessions, presentation mock-ups, networking parties, etc.

This website serves as an archive of past symposium projects and researches which have been granted display permission by their rightful creators. We hope you find this website to be an useful tool to jumpstart your very own project and get a feel of the standards for OCC symposium submissions.
